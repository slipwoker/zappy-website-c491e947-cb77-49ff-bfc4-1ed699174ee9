How We Guide Your Journey

A simple, reassuring path from your first thought of white river kayaking & outdoors to riding with confidence. Every step is built around your level and the day's conditions.

Meet Your Level

We start with a quick chat and a look at your experience. Whether you're a complete beginner or already comfortable with kite control, we'll match you to the right lesson plan or guided trip.

Read Wind & Spot Conditions

Before you touch the water, we'll teach you to understand the wind window, tide, and local weather. This knowledge is the foundation of safe, thrilling sessions and is part of every Alexi experience.

Build Confidence on Water

With a clear plan and real-time feedback, you'll progress from body dragging to water starts and sustained rides. Our instructors stay close, using radio helmets so you feel supported at every moment.

Continue with Guided Progression

After your session, we'll map out your next steps. Whether it's mastering transitions, learning to jump, or joining a downwinder trip, your progression stays on track with the Alexi community behind you.

Why Ride with Alexi

We believe kitesurfing is more than a sport — it's a connection to wind, water, and wild places. Our lessons are built around you, the environment, and the unforgettable feeling of gliding across the sea.

Personal Coaching

One-on-one guidance tailored to your skill level, from first water start to advanced tricks. Our certified instructors focus on safety, progression, and pure stoke.

Environmental Respect

We ride with deep respect for the ocean. Every lesson includes awareness of local ecosystems, tides, and sustainable practices to protect the playground we love.

Memorable Immersion

Beyond the lesson, it's an experience. Feel the salt spray, watch the sunset from the water, and take home stories that last long after the wind dies down.
