Born from wind, shaped by the sea

Alexi isn't just a kitesurfing brand — it's a way of moving through the world. We believe the best adventures begin where the ocean meets the sky, and that every gust of wind carries the promise of something extraordinary. Our story is written in salt spray and sand, in the quiet moments before dawn when the tide is right and the breeze begins to stir.

Rooted in a deep respect for the elements, we craft experiences that feel both thrilling and grounded. Whether you're chasing your first ride or perfecting a freestyle trick, Alexi is here to guide you — with integrity, passion, and a genuine love for the wild, beautiful world of white river kayaking & outdoors.

Our Core Values

Respect for Wind & Water

We believe the ocean is our greatest teacher. Every session at Alexi starts with understanding the elements — reading the wind, respecting the tide, and moving in harmony with nature rather than against it. This reverence shapes every lesson, every trip, and every piece of advice we share.

Honest Progression Over Hype

We don't promise overnight miracles. Real progression on the water comes from deliberate practice, expert coaching, and a supportive community. At Alexi, we celebrate the small wins — your first water start, your first upwind ride — because we know that lasting confidence is built step by step, not through empty marketing.

Adventure with Responsibility

Thrill-seeking means nothing without safety. We equip every guest with the skills, gear checks, and environmental awareness needed to explore confidently. From our white river kayaking & outdoors expeditions to coastal kitesurfing camps, we ensure that adventure and responsibility go hand in hand — so you can push your limits without compromising your wellbeing.
