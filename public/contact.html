Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session with Alexi

Who are the sessions suitable for?

Our sessions are designed for everyone — from complete beginners taking their first steps into kitesurfing to experienced riders looking to refine advanced techniques. We tailor every session to your individual skill level and comfort in the water.

What should I bring with me?

We provide all the essential gear including kites, boards, harnesses, and safety equipment. You just need to bring swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, and plenty of energy. A change of warm clothes for after the session is always a good idea.

How does weather affect the planning?

Wind and weather are at the heart of kitesurfing. We monitor conditions daily and will confirm your session 24 hours in advance. If conditions aren't safe or suitable, we'll work with you to reschedule at no extra cost. Flexibility is key to the best experience.

Are complete beginners welcome?

Absolutely. Most of our guests start with zero experience. Our beginner courses cover everything from safety theory and kite control on the beach to your first board starts in the water. You'll be amazed at how quickly you progress with the right guidance.

What about safety and insurance?

Safety is our top priority. All instructors are internationally certified and carry first-aid training. We use premium, regularly inspected equipment. We recommend you have personal travel and activity insurance that covers water sports.

How long is a typical session?

A standard session lasts around 2 to 3 hours including setup, briefing, and on-water time. We find this gives the perfect balance of learning and enjoyment without fatigue. Half-day and full-day packages are also available for those wanting a deeper dive.
