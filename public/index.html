Why Ride With Alexi

We combine safety, skill-building, and memorable coastal sessions to create a genuine kitesurfing experience far beyond generic tourism. Every session is crafted around you, the wind, and the water.

Beginner-Friendly Progression

From your first body drag to your first independent ride, our structured approach builds confidence safely. We break down every skill into manageable steps so you progress at your own pace without feeling overwhelmed.

Local Wind & Water Knowledge

Our instructors live and breathe these coastal conditions. We know exactly when the thermals kick in, where the flat-water lagoons hide, and how to read the gusts — giving you the safest and most exhilarating ride every session.

Small-Group Adventure Energy

Forget crowded tourist classes. We keep groups intentionally small to maximize your time on the water, foster real camaraderie, and tailor coaching to your individual style. It's not just a lesson — it's a shared adventure.

Where Wind Meets Water

Three distinct experiences, each a chapter of the ocean. From your first powered ride to mastering open-water swells, discover the session that matches your spirit.

Guided Downwind Coastal Rides

Follow the coastline on a curated downwinder. You'll ride wind shadows past hidden coves and sea stacks, with a support boat tracking your line. Perfect for intermediate riders craving distance and discovery.

Open-Water Lessons

Start where the wind is steady and the water is flat. Our certified instructors use radio helmets and jet-ski support so you progress safely from body dragging to your first board starts.

Weekend Escape Sessions

Disconnect completely. We handle logistics, tides, and wind forecasts so you can focus on riding. Camp under the stars, wake up to offshore breeze, and ride empty lineups all weekend.
